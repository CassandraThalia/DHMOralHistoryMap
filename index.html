About the Listening Map

This map features audio clips from the Dartmouth Heritage Museum's oral history collection. They were recorded in the 1980s and 90s by members of the Dartmouth Museum Society. All of the interviewees are Dartmouthians who survived the Dartmouth/Halifax explosion of 1917, which remains the second largest manmade explosion in history.

To learn more about the Darmouth/Halifax Explosion, come visit our exhibit at historic Evergreen House.
